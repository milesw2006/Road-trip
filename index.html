Flights to Denver — Philly Area Airports
PHL (Philadelphia) · EWR (Newark) → DEN (Denver) · ~45 min drive to Boulder
💡 Denver (DEN) is your best bet — direct flights from PHL, only 45 min drive to Boulder. June is actually one of the cheaper months to fly this route. Frontier and Southwest are consistently the cheapest. Book 4–8 weeks out for the best price. Avoid checked bags on Frontier/Spirit — they'll add $40–70 each way.
